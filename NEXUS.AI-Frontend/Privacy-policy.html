What We Collect
& Why.

Every data point has a specific, documented purpose. No speculation, no profiling.

01Information We Collect

We collect only what is genuinely necessary to provide our services, maintain security, and fulfil legal obligations.

  • Account information: name, email address, and organisational details provided at signup
  • Usage data: page visits, feature interactions, and error logs — anonymised and aggregated
  • Payment information: processed by our PCI-DSS certified processor; we never store raw card numbers
  • Support communications: messages sent through our help channels, retained only while your case is open

02How We Use Your Information

Every data point we collect has a specific, documented purpose. We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or resale under any circumstance.

  • To authenticate your identity and protect your account from unauthorised access
  • To deliver, maintain, and continuously improve our platform
  • To send transactional communications — invoices, security alerts, critical service updates
  • To comply with applicable law, court orders, or valid government requests

03Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as required to fulfil the purpose it was collected for, or as required by law. When an account is deleted, all associated personal data is permanently purged within 30 days — including backups and archives.

04Third-Party Sub-processors

We work with a small number of carefully vetted sub-processors. Each is bound by data processing agreements that prohibit secondary use of your data.

  • Cloud infrastructure: ISO 27001-certified hosting with dedicated tenancy options available
  • Payment processing: PCI-DSS Level 1 certified; zero raw card data stored by us
  • Email delivery: transactional emails only, with strict data minimisation clauses

05Cookies & Tracking

We use only strictly necessary cookies to maintain session state and user preferences. We do not deploy third-party tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or cross-site fingerprinting technologies. Your browsing habits are yours alone.
