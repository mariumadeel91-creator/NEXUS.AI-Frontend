Common Questions
When do I get paid?
Payouts happen monthly (Starter), weekly (Pro), or daily (Elite) via bank transfer, PayPal, or Wise. Minimum payout threshold is $50.
How long does the cookie last?
Your referral cookie lasts 90 days. If someone clicks your link and subscribes within 90 days, you earn commission — even if they came back directly.
Do I earn on plan upgrades?
Yes! Commission is calculated on the actual plan value. If your referral upgrades from Pro ($79) to Enterprise ($199), your earnings increase automatically that same billing cycle.
Can I refer my own company?
Self-referrals are not permitted and will be flagged by our fraud detection system. Affiliate accounts found self-referring will be permanently suspended.
What happens if a referral cancels?
Commission stops when a referred company cancels. If they resubscribe within 60 days, you resume earning. After 60 days, the attribution resets.
Is there a limit on how much I can earn?
No cap, no limit. Some of our top affiliates earn over $10,000/month. The more companies you refer, the more you earn — with no ceiling.

Quick Start
Query the AI Brain in 3 lines

The most common use case — ask your company's AI Brain a policy question and get a structured answer.

const response = await fetch('https://api.nexus.ai/v2/brain/query', {
  method: 'POST',
  headers: {
    'Authorization': 'Bearer nxs_live_your_api_key',
    'Content-Type': 'application/json',
  },
  body: JSON.stringify({
    question: 'What is the escalation procedure when a customer complaint is not resolved in 24 hours?',
    employee_id: 'emp_7k2m9p',
    language: 'en',
  }),
});
const data = await response.json();
console.log(data.answer);  // "According to SOP-12 section 4.2, escalate to..."
console.log(data.sources); // ["Customer_Complaint_SOP_v3.pdf", "HR_Policy_Manual.docx"]
import requests

response = requests.post(
    "https://api.nexus.ai/v2/brain/query",
    headers={
        "Authorization": "Bearer nxs_live_your_api_key",
        "Content-Type": "application/json",
    },
    json={
        "question": "What is the escalation procedure for unresolved complaints?",
        "employee_id": "emp_7k2m9p",
        "language": "en",
    }
)
data = response.json()
print(data["answer"])   # According to SOP-12 section 4.2...
print(data["sources"]) # ["Customer_Complaint_SOP_v3.pdf"]
curl -X POST https://api.nexus.ai/v2/brain/query \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nxs_live_your_api_key" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "question": "What is the escalation procedure for unresolved complaints?",
    "employee_id": "emp_7k2m9p",
    "language": "en"
  }'
